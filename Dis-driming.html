„Раздримување- колективна игра“
"Dis-driming- collective game"

..they engage in an experimental and explorative approach to creating visual sensations that allow for diverse perceptions and create specific experiences. The work as a whole is a collaborative installation project designed at a specific location. Throughout the process of creating the work, the artists are guided and inspired by the specificity of the natural materials: cane, stone, water, sludge. The focus of interest is on exploring, re-examining the importance of the natural world heritage in the human-nature relation.
Artists: Goran Ristovski, Filip Velkovski Viktorija Poposka, Sali Ertunc, Milko Nestoroski
Locaion: Struga, R. North Macedonia
