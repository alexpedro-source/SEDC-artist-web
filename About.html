GORAN RISTOVSKI

...born in Struga, R.Macedonia, graduated from the Ss. Cyril and Methodius University, where he went to the Faculty of Fine Arts - Skopje and majored in graphic art with department in graphic design. He has participated in several group exhibitions in Macedonia and abroad. My artistic practice is concerned with exploring of different media like installation, graphic arts, street- urban art, painting, drawing, digital art, photography but most often it is a combination of media.
